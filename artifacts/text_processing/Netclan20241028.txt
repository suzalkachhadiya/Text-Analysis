the problem
our client a leading financial institution faced the challenge of leveraging machine learning for predictive analytics while ensuring the security and privacy of sensitive data traditional machine learning models posed limitations in preserving data privacy especially when dealing with sensitive financial information
our solution
we proposed integrating fully homomorphic encryption fhe with the powerful xgboost model to provide secure and privacypreserving predictive analytics capabilities by implementing fhexgboost we aimed to enable the client to leverage machine learning for decisionmaking without compromising data privacy
